Border Run Checklist

Documents for Thailand border runs. Preparation checklist for extending your stay through border crossings.

What to Prepare

A border run is a trip to a neighbouring country to exit Thailand and return on a new visa-exempt entry or tourist visa. Popular crossings from northern Thailand include Laos (Vientiane, Udon Thani), Myanmar, and Cambodia. This checklist covers essentials for a successful border run.

Preparation Status 0/8

Disclaimer: Border run legality and immigration enforcement varies. While border runs are technically allowed, Thai immigration has discretion to deny re-entry or limit repeated visa-exempt entries. Verify current border crossing procedures and requirements with your target border crossing before departure. Have required visas or proof of eligibility for neighbouring countries. This checklist is a guide only and not a substitute for official requirements or legal advice.
