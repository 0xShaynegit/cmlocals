What to Prepare

The DTV (Digital Nomad) visa is designed for remote workers and freelancers earning foreign income. Valid for 180 days per entry and renewable annually. You must demonstrate foreign income of at least 1.8 million THB per year or comparable financial support.

Preparation Status 0/10

Disclaimer: Thai immigration requirements for the DTV visa are relatively new and subject to change. Income thresholds, required documentation, and acceptable proof of employment vary between embassies and immigration offices. Verify current requirements with Thai immigration or your nearest Thai embassy before applying. This checklist is a guide only and not a substitute for official documentation.
