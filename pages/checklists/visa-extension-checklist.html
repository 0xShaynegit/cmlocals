What to Prepare

Extending a long-stay visa (Non-O, Non-B, ED, DTV, etc.) requires specific documents based on your visa type and purpose. File your extension 7-14 days before your current visa expires. Use this checklist to gather everything you need before visiting immigration.

💡 Pro Tip: When you receive your extension stamp, write down the expiry date

📅
Never Miss Your Visa Expiry
When immigration stamps your extension approval, enter your visa expiry date to automatically add it to your calendar with reminders for 7 days, 2 days, and day-of.
Example: 15/06/2026
