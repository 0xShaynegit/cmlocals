What to Prepare

A Re-entry Permit preserves your active visa while you leave Thailand. Without one, your visa is automatically cancelled when you exit the country. Re-entry Permits are single-entry (renewed each time you leave) or multiple-entry (valid for one year). This checklist covers the documents needed to obtain one.

Preparation Status 0/7

Disclaimer: Without a Re-entry Permit, your Thai visa is cancelled when you leave Thailand. Re-entry Permits must be applied for before departure at Thai immigration offices. Requirements vary slightly by immigration office. Verify current requirements with your local immigration office before applying. This checklist is a guide only and not a substitute for official requirements.
