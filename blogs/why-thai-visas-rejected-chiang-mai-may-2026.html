Why Are Thai Visas Being Rejected in Chiang Mai? (May 2026 Update)

By CMLocals Team • Published May 2026 • 8 min read
  1. Home
  2. /
  3. Blog
  4. /
  5. Why Are Thai Visas Being Rejected in Chiang Mai? (May 2026 Update)

Thai visa rejections in Chiang Mai have shifted dramatically since January 2026. Rejections are now driven by digital mismatches, source verification, and automated attendance tracking. This guide explains the 2026 rejection landscape and how to avoid pitfalls.

TM30 Mismatches: The Digital Problem

The most common rejection reason in May 2026 is TM30 data mismatch. Chiang Mai Immigration now cross-references TM30 digital records (submitted by landlords) against internal immigration databases. If your registered address does not match the address you submitted in your visa application, your request is rejected.

This is not intentional fraud on your part. Many landlords delay TM30 filing or register the wrong unit. Before you apply, confirm that your landlord has filed the TM30 and that the address is correct. Request a copy of the filed TM30 confirmation.

Key Point

Always request written confirmation from your landlord that the TM30 has been filed with your correct address. Mismatches are now the top reason for visa rejections.

DTV Financial Source Verification

The DTV visa requires proof of income or savings. Since May 2026, Chiang Mai Immigration has been verifying source documents directly with banks and employers. Bank statements that lack official stamps or watermarks are now commonly rejected. Employer letters that cannot be verified with the company are flagged.

The key change is that officers now call or email the issuing bank and employer to confirm the documents are real. If you use a freelance income source or gig-economy work, ensure you have bank statements that clearly show income deposits and official bank certification.

ED Visa Attendance Tracking

ED visa renewals and extensions are now subject to automated attendance verification. Schools submit class attendance records to the Ministry of Education database. If your record shows gaps in attendance (particularly during periods when you were supposed to be in class), your ED visa renewal can be rejected.

This affects language students, martial arts students, and IT certification students most. If you take a week off for travel, ensure it is aligned with school holidays or obtain written approval from your school before you leave.

Pro Tip

For ED visas, maintain attendance consistently. If you need to miss class, inform your school in advance and obtain written confirmation of the absence reason. Excused absences are not flagged in the automated system.

Takeaway

May 2026 rejections are data-driven, not discretionary. The shifts are TM30 digital verification, DTV financial source verification, and ED visa automated attendance tracking. The path to approval is documentation and transparency. If you are aware of potential issues (TM30 delays, freelance income, school absences), address them before you apply. Chiang Mai Immigration is now equipped to verify claims across multiple systems.
