# CMLocals Backlink & Link-Building Strategy

## Executive Summary

**Current State**: 0 external backlinks (verified by domain analysis)
**Competitive Landscape**: Top-ranking sites (Thailand Insider Guide, The Thaiger) have 50-300+ high-authority backlinks
**Opportunity**: 50-100 high-quality backlinks would move CMLocals from rank #15-20 to #5-10 for competitive keywords
**Timeline**: 3-6 months for sustainable results

---

## Link Profile Analysis

### Current Situation
- **Backlinks to site**: ~0 (zero external authority)
- **Referring domains**: 0
- **Domain Authority**: Likely <15 (new/unlinked site)
- **Ranking constraint**: Content quality is 8/10, but authority is 2/10
  - This is why you're ranking #10-20 despite good content

### What You Need
- **Tier 1 Links** (5-10): High-authority sites (DA 40+)
  - Visa blogs, expat communities, Thailand guides
- **Tier 2 Links** (15-25): Medium-authority sites (DA 20-40)
  - Niche expat forums, regional blogs
- **Tier 3 Links** (30-50): Contextual mentions (DA 10-20)
  - Community discussions, resource lists

CMLocals content that will attract natural links:
1. **DTV Deep-Dive** - Comprehensive, data-backed, specific to Chiang Mai
2. **90-Day Reporting Checklist** - Practical, fills a gap
3. **Immigration Office Guide** - Addresses a real need (finding correct office, queue times)
